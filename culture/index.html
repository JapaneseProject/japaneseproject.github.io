Education

To the Japanese, the love of learning is an important virtue of a man's life.

Eduation is a big part of Japanese culture.

Education isn't focused on critical thinking, it's more focused on test taking.

If parents find the schooling unsatisfactory, they can get a private tutor or send their kids to private schools.

Formal education didn't come to Japan until the Chinese language system, which was adopted in 500 CE. Before then, only the rich could afford education.
