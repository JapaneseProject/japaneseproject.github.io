Japan Overall

The country of Japan is located east of china and consists of an archipelago that contains 4 main islands and thousands of other smaller ones. The islands Honshu, Hokkaido, Kyushu and Shikoku have varying geography and weather. Overall, the land is very mountainous and the temperature is warm and humid.

Hokkaido

Hokkaido is the most northern island and has a cold climate. The average temperature annually is a low 10 degrees celsius. Especially during winter, it gets very cold and there is heavy snowfall. It is located very close to Russia, and is surrounded by The Sea of Japan, Sea of Okhotsk, and the Pacific Ocean. Fun Fact: This islnd is known for its Ramen!
